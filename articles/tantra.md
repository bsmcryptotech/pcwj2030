In modern day America, there are three major classifications of Tantra.
Spiritual (Authentic/Traditional) Tantra
Practices strictly based on ancient religions and philosophies (Hindu/Buddhist).
Heavy reliance on the assistance of gods and goddesses for superhuman powers.
Has nothing to do with sexual intercourse for the sake of pleasure.
Can be traced back through a lineage.
Physical (Western/Neo) Tantra
Practices loosely based on Theosophical/New Age belief systems.
Mostly atheist in nature.
Is mainly a form of exotic, sexual foreplay.
Can be traced back to the 1960s "Free Love" sexual revolution.
Metaphysical (Experiential/Alchemical) Tantra
Practices based on principles of moving energy within individual physical bodies using ancient and cutting-edge methods.
Leverages spiritual concepts without invoking any deities.
Advanced alchemy involves sex magic rituals that are used to manifest tangible benefits.
Can be traced back to the early 2020s.

Vedic Fusion Tantra is a hybrid metaphysical system that embodies the nexus of ancient wisdom and modern sensual alchemy.
As practitioners, we use breathwork, healing sound frequencies, meditation, yoga, and mudras to transmute our energies to manifest greatness.
We also connect with each other through various forms of sacred touch to optimize our human capabilities.
Wisdom Touch Tantra comprised of a unique set of paths based on classical tantric perspectives.
Practitioners not only enjoy the benefits of self-improvement and energetic alignment, they are able to contribute to a reservoir of living energy that can be accessed in future incarnations.
